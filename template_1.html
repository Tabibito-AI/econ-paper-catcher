Education is considered, in every sense, as one of the fundamental factors of development. No country can achieve sustainable development and economic growth without a systematic education system. The Impact of Remote Work on Educational Outcomes: A Study of Online Learning During the Global Pandemic

This study examines the effects of remote work and online learning on educational outcomes during the global pandemic. Using a comprehensive dataset from multiple educational institutions, we analyze the adaptation patterns of students and educators to virtual learning environments. Our findings indicate significant variations in learning outcomes across different socioeconomic groups and geographical locations.
